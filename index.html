Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi AKA Kid Cudi was born January 30, 1984. He is from Cleavland, Ohio but grew up in Shaker Heights and Solon. He is a perosn of many talens from being a rapper/singer and actor. He is the founder of Mad Solar and Wicked Awesome Records. He is also a father to one child, Vada Mescudi.

Cudi rose to fame in 2007 when he dropped his hit song Day 'n' Nite. His work caught the attention of Kanye West who would later bring him on board and have him sign to GOOD Music. Kid Cudi would then release his first mixtape, A Kid Named Cudi in 2008. He would also find more success later on in 2008 when he would go on to help and appear on Kanye's hit album 808s & Heartbreak. The following year Kid Cudi would release his first studio album, Man on the Moon. This album would lead to a triology happening. His first studio album would go on to recieve three Grammy Awards nominations.

people believe Kid Cudi brought a new sound to hip-hop. His music was something different from the normal sound of hip-hop. It would fuse different styles of music such as electornica and indie pop. It brought a spacey vibe to the album and made listeners feel like they were on a journey with Kid Cudi through his mind and emotions he was dealing with. Kid cudi's lyrics would be very different from other hip-hop artists. He would talk about his own emtions and struggles in his music. He helped a genereation of teens and adults dealing with similar emotions and struggles.

Kid Cudi also acts from time to time. He appeared in a few films ranging from Need for Speed to Don't Look Up. He would later come out in a film called X. He executive produced and acted in the film. He has been taking acting more seriously due to him appearing and acting in more films. His acting carrer is just taking off. He is directing a film that is in post-production and appearing in another film that is also in post-production.
